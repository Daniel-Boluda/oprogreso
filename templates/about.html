About Playlist Downloader

Welcome to Playlist Downloader, a Django-based web application that simplifies the process of downloading YouTube videos and integrating them into your music collection in Navidrome.

Whether you're a music enthusiast or a casual listener, Playlist Downloader provides you with a convenient way to create playlists, download YouTube videos, and seamlessly add them to your Navidrome library.

With Playlist Downloader, you can easily discover new music on YouTube and enjoy it in your preferred music player without any hassle.
